Payment Guide

Follow these simple steps to complete your payment. Choose the method that works best for you.

GCash

Fast and secure mobile payment

How to Pay:

  1. Open your GCash app
  2. Select "Send Money"
  3. Enter our GCash number: 0917-123-4567
  4. Enter the exact amount
  5. Click Send and confirm
  6. Take a screenshot of your receipt
  7. Keep your reference number for verification

Make sure to save your transaction reference number for confirmation.

ECPay

Convenient payment centers nationwide

How to Pay:

  1. Visit any ECPay partner outlet (7-Eleven, SM, Robinsons)
  2. Tell the cashier you want to make an ECPay payment
  3. Provide biller name: ICCT Colleges
  4. Give your student number as reference
  5. Pay the exact amount in cash
  6. Get your official receipt
  7. Keep the receipt with reference number

ECPay is available at 7-Eleven, SM Business Centers, Robinsons, and other partner outlets.

Cebuana Lhuillier

Pay at any Cebuana Lhuillier branch

How to Pay:

  1. Go to any Cebuana Lhuillier branch
  2. Fill out the payment form
  3. Recipient: ICCT Colleges - Antipolo
  4. Write your student number and name
  5. Pay the amount in cash
  6. Get the official receipt
  7. Keep the control number for tracking

Present a valid ID and your student ID when making payments.

M Lhuillier

Remittance center payment option

How to Pay:

  1. Visit any M Lhuillier branch nationwide
  2. Request for "Bills Payment"
  3. Company name: ICCT Colleges
  4. Account name: ICCT Antipolo Campus
  5. Provide your student number as reference
  6. Pay the exact amount in cash
  7. Secure your receipt and reference number

M Lhuillier has branches in malls, public markets, and major areas nationwide.

Important: Always keep your payment receipts and reference numbers. Payment processing takes 24-48 hours. For inquiries, contact the cashier's office at (02) 1234-5678.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using the ICCT Store. Find answers to common questions below.

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click the profile icon in the top right corner, then click "Sign In" and select "Create New Account". Fill in your student number, email, and create a password. Your account will be ready to use immediately!

Do I need an account to browse products?

No! You can browse all products without an account. However, you'll need to sign in to add items to cart, save favorites, and track your orders.

Shopping & Orders

How do I place an order?

Browse products, click "Add to Cart", review your cart, click "Checkout", complete payment following the payment guide, and your order will be processed!

Can I modify my order after placing it?

Orders can be modified within 24 hours of placement. Visit the Cashier's Office or call (02) 1234-5678 to request changes.

How do I track my order?

Log in to your account and click on "Transaction History" to see all your orders and their current status (Processing, Ready, Completed).

Payment

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept GCash, Maya, Bank Transfer, Over the Counter (cash/card), and Installment Plans. See the Payment Guide for detailed instructions.

Is installment payment available?

Yes! Installment plans (2-6 months) are available for purchases over ₱5,000. Visit the Cashier's Office with your valid ID and student ID to apply.

When will my payment be confirmed?

Digital payments (GCash, Maya, Bank Transfer) are confirmed within 1-2 hours. Over-the-counter payments are confirmed immediately.

Products & Availability

What if my size is out of stock?

Contact the Cashier's Office at (02) 1234-5678. We can usually order your size, which typically arrives within 5-7 business days.

Can I return or exchange products?

Uniforms and clothing can be exchanged within 7 days if unworn with tags attached. Documents and fees are non-refundable. Bring your receipt to the Cashier's Office.

Documents & Fees

How long does document processing take?

Most documents are ready in 3-5 business days. Certificates of Enrollment: 1-2 days. Transcripts: 5-7 days. Official documents: 7-10 days. Check the Supplies Info for details.

Where do I claim my documents?

All documents can be claimed at the Registrar's Office during office hours (Mon-Fri, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM). Bring your student ID and payment receipt.

Support

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Visit the Cashier's Office with your student ID to reset your password. For security reasons, password resets must be done in person.

Who do I contact for help?

For payment issues: Cashier's Office - (02) 1234-5678
For documents: Registrar's Office - (02) 1234-5679
For technical support: Email icctstore@icct.edu.ph
Office Hours: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Still have questions? Visit the Cashier's Office at ICCT Antipolo Campus or call (02) 1234-5678 during office hours.
