Payment Methods

Choose from our secure and convenient payment methods. Follow the instructions below for each payment option.

GCash

Fast and secure mobile payment

How to Pay:

  1. Open your GCash app
  2. Select "Send Money" or "Pay QR"
  3. Enter our GCash number: 0917-123-4567
  4. Enter the amount and send
  5. Take a screenshot of the receipt
  6. Email the receipt to: payment@icct.edu.ph

Maya (PayMaya)

Digital wallet payment

How to Pay:

  1. Open Maya app
  2. Tap "Send Money"
  3. Select "To Mobile Number"
  4. Enter: 0918-765-4321
  5. Enter amount and confirm
  6. Save the transaction receipt
  7. Send receipt to: payment@icct.edu.ph

Bank Transfer

Direct bank deposit or online transfer

How to Pay:

  1. Go to your bank or use online banking
  2. Transfer to: BDO - ICCT Colleges
  3. Account Number: 0012-3456-7890
  4. Enter the amount
  5. Keep the deposit slip or confirmation
  6. Email to: payment@icct.edu.ph
  7. Include your name and student number

Over the Counter

Pay at the campus cashier

How to Pay:

  1. Visit ICCT Antipolo Campus Cashier
  2. Office Hours: Mon-Fri, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  3. Bring your student ID
  4. Present your order details
  5. Pay in cash or card
  6. Get your official receipt
  7. Items can be claimed after payment

Credit/Debit Card

Visa, Mastercard, JCB accepted

How to Pay:

  1. Click "Pay with Card" button
  2. Enter your card details
  3. Card Number, Expiry, CVV
  4. Enter billing information
  5. Review your order
  6. Click "Complete Payment"
  7. Save the confirmation email

Installment Plan

Pay in flexible installments

How to Apply:

  1. Visit the Cashier's Office
  2. Bring valid ID and student ID
  3. Fill out installment form
  4. Choose payment schedule (2-6 months)
  5. Submit required documents
  6. Wait for approval (1-2 days)
  7. Start paying monthly installments

Important: Please keep all payment receipts and transaction confirmations. For any payment concerns, contact us at payment@icct.edu.ph or call (02) 1234-5678.
